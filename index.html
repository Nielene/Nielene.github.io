So, why did I choose to be a Web Developer? Look around! It is a world within a world. For centuries, people have lived off of stories. Ones they made with their lives and those they lived vicariously. Stories can be used to excite, or to soothe. They are used to make someone see and feel what you need them to. And, yes, they are used to sell and market. A website is the newest story canvas. The colors, the curve, and the positioning of an evocative picture make us feel. The words are a second aspect of a website. Please, click a link, and feel my Art of Website Design.

For some time, people have trusted me to tell the story of their companies - who they are, what they give to the world, and all they plan to do in the future. Their impact on their customers is central to any site I build. Click on Projects above and join my customers.
